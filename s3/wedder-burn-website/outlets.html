Outlets / Switches / Fixtures upgrade

Upgrade the functionality and aesthetics of your home's electrical components with Who Wedderburn Electric. Outdated outlets, switches, and light fixtures not only detract from the appearance of your home but can also pose safety hazards and limit functionality. Our experienced electricians can enhance your home's electrical system by upgrading outdated components to modern, energy-efficient alternatives.

Whether you're looking to replace worn-out outlets and switches, install dimmer switches for adjustable lighting, or upgrade to stylish and efficient light fixtures, we have the expertise to handle all your upgrade needs. We begin by assessing your current electrical setup and discussing your preferences and budget.

Then, we recommend energy-efficient solutions and handle all aspects of the installation with precision and attention to detail. Trust us to ensure your upgraded components operate safely and reliably, enhancing the comfort and functionality of your home.
